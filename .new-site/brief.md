# Brand Brief

## Positioning
- **Business:** Brackstone Digital — full-stack digital growth partner for UK small businesses (cold email outreach, voice AI, website builds, paid ads)
- **Ideal Customer:** UK SMB owner/director (1-50 employees), local/regional business, needs more customers but doesn't have a marketing team. Not technical.
- **Primary CTA:** Book a 15-minute discovery call
- **Differentiator:** One partner for the full stack — outreach, AI, web, ads — instead of juggling 4 separate agencies or freelancers
- **Positioning Statement:** Brackstone Digital helps small UK businesses get more customers by combining cold email outreach, AI voice receptionists, conversion-focused websites, and paid advertising into one done-for-you service. We're the growth partner that SMBs wish they'd found sooner.

## Brand Voice & Tone
- **Personality:** Warm, capable, straightforward
- **Tone:** Conversational but competent — like a knowledgeable friend, not a sales pitch
- **We are:** Approachable, results-focused, practical, honest
- **We are NOT:** Corporate, jargon-heavy, intimidating, flashy without substance

## Visual Identity

### Color Constraints
None — fresh palette, designer's choice per concept. Moving away from navy+teal.

### Font Constraints
None — designer's choice per concept. Moving away from Inter/DM Sans.

> Final palette and fonts are selected per concept direction during brainstorm.

### Visual Style
- **Keywords:** Warm, approachable, trustworthy, clean, human
- **Inspiration patterns:** No specific URLs provided — student trusts designer judgment
- **Do:** Make it feel friendly and easy to engage with. Show capability without overwhelming.
- **Don't:** Stuff the page with services lists. No fake testimonials. No stock photos of handshakes.
