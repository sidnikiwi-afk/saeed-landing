# Your New Site Plan

## Brand Brief
Brackstone Digital is a full-stack digital growth partner for UK small businesses. You combine cold email outreach, voice AI receptionists, website builds, and paid ads into one done-for-you service. Your ideal client is a UK SMB owner (1-50 employees) who needs more customers but doesn't have a marketing team. The primary CTA is booking a free 15-minute discovery call. Your differentiator: one partner for the full stack instead of juggling 4 agencies.

## Visual Direction
- Color/font constraints: None — fresh palette per concept, moving away from navy+teal/Inter+DM Sans
- Vibe: Warm, approachable, trustworthy, capable, human

## Site Blueprint
### Homepage (5 sections)
1. **Hero** — Headline + subline + "Book a free call" CTA
2. **Problem** — 3-4 pain points SMBs face (juggling tools, wasting money, no marketing team)
3. **How It Works** — 3-step process (chat → we build → you grow)
4. **Capabilities** — 4 services shown as one integrated approach, not a services list
5. **Final CTA** — Closing headline + book a call button

No testimonials, case studies, or client logos (none available yet).

## Concept Directions

### Direction 1: Friendly Ground
- **Palette:** Terracotta #C4724E, Sage #7A9E7E, Warm Sand #D4A574 on Cream #FBF8F4
- **Fonts:** Quicksand + Outfit
- Spacious, pill-shaped buttons, organic SVG curve dividers, typographic hero

### Direction 2: Neon Pulse
- **Palette:** Electric Lime #BFFF00, Indigo #16213E, Cyan #00D4AA on Off-Black #0F0F1A
- **Fonts:** Sora + Manrope
- Dark theme, sharp edges, angled dividers, bento grid, glowing hover states

### Direction 3: Open Book
- **Palette:** Warm Coral #E8725A, Slate Navy #2C3E50, Soft Gold #E8C87A on Warm White #FEFCFA
- **Fonts:** Fraunces + Nunito Sans
- Editorial serif headings, split-screen hero, alternating layouts, storytelling flow
